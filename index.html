About Me

I'm a PhD student in mathematics at Heriot-Watt University, Maxwell Institute for Mathematical Sciences. My supervisor is Prof. Lorenzo Pareschi. I recevied my Bachelor's degree at Seoul National University, supervised by Prof. Sunsig Byun. I am interested in stochastic particle system and its relation to optimization method, kinetic theory.
